Hi! I'm Amy. I like to create things.

In my philosophical studies I create connections and ideas. Following Mary Midgley, I think the work of the philosopher is to unclog concepts (like a plumber) and help people develop better ways of thinking. To this end, I create podcasts to make philosophical ideas more accessible to the public.

In addition to my philosophical work, I like learning and creating in all sorts of spheres. I taught myself some basic html so that I could create this website from scratch!
