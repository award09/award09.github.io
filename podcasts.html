Podcasting

I have created & produced three podcasts (with help). The first was a pet-project with my sister (no longer available online). The second & third podcasts came into being thanks to student research grants in 2019 & 2020 at Mount Allison University.

Listen to Convinced, a podcast about religious belief featuring interviews with several Christian leaders in and around Sackville, New Brunswick.

Listen to the In Parenthesis Podcast, made in collaboration with Women in Parenthesis, about the philosophy of four important thinkers in the 20thc: Mary Midgley, Iris Murdoch, Philippa Foot, & Elizabeth Anscombe.
